üî¨ Three-Hypothesis Framework

Hypothesis 1: Discriminative Validity

Question: Can the framework distinguish dignity from tribalism rhetoric?

Criteria:

  • Dignity texts should score >0.6 on Dignity well, <0.4 on Tribalism well
  • Tribalism texts should score >0.6 on Tribalism well, <0.4 on Dignity well
  • Mixed texts should score 0.4-0.6 on both wells
  • Statistical significance p<0.05 between groups

Hypothesis 2: Ideological Agnosticism

Question: Does the framework score content rather than political orientation?

Criteria:

  • Conservative dignity should score similarly to progressive dignity
  • Conservative tribalism should score similarly to progressive tribalism
  • No significant difference between ideological groups within same category

Hypothesis 3: Ground Truth Alignment

Question: Do pre-labeled texts match expected framework responses?

Criteria:

  • Validates our manual categorization process
  • Extreme controls should show strongest expected patterns
  • Framework should align with human expert judgment
