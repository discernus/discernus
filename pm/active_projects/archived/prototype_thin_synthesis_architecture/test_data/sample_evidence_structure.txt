EVIDENCE CSV STRUCTURE:

Columns:
- artifact_id: Unique identifier matching scores CSV
- dimension: Which analytical dimension this evidence supports
- evidence_text: Direct quote from the source text
- context: Surrounding context for the quote
- confidence: Float 0.0-1.0, confidence in this evidence
- reasoning: Brief explanation of why this supports the dimension

Sample rows: ~200-1000 evidence entries across all artifacts
Expected format: CSV with headers, some fields may be empty
Multiple evidence entries per artifact_id are common
